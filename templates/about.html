ABOUT

THE BAND

Formed in the heart of Regensburg, Germany in 2021, Smokin' Breadsticks has quickly risen to become one of the most exciting indie rock bands in the local scene. With their unique blend of garage rock, punk energy, and indie sensibilities, the band has crafted a sound that's both familiar and refreshingly original.

The band's name, inspired by a late-night post-gig snack, perfectly captures their approach to music: comfort food with a kick. Their live performances are known for high energy, tight musicianship, and an undeniable connection with the audience.

After releasing their debut EP "Marinara Dreams" in 2023, which garnered attention from local radio and music blogs, the band followed up with their first full-length album "Dough Rising" in early 2024. The album's lead single "Garlic Knots" has become their signature song, showcasing their knack for catchy hooks and clever lyrics.

Having shared stages with established acts across Germany and building a dedicated fanbase through relentless touring, Smokin' Breadsticks is poised to break through to the wider European music scene with their upcoming summer tour.

MEET THE BAND

Max Schmidt

Lead Vocals & Guitar

The frontman with a voice that can shift from melodic crooning to raw power in an instant. Max founded the band after years of playing solo acoustic shows around Regensburg. His songwriting forms the backbone of the Smokin' Breadsticks sound.

Favorite breadstick: Garlic Parmesan

Lukas Weber

Lead Guitar

The guitar virtuoso whose riffs and solos add the perfect amount of edge to the band's sound. Lukas studied jazz guitar before finding his true calling in rock. His technical skill and creative approach to the instrument elevate every song.

Favorite breadstick: Rosemary & Sea Salt

Anna Müller

Bass & Backing Vocals

The rhythmic foundation of the band with a melodic approach to bass playing that adds depth to every track. Anna's harmonies with Max create the signature vocal sound of the band. Previously played in several punk bands before joining Smokin' Breadsticks.

Favorite breadstick: Jalapeño Cheese

Thomas Becker

Drums & Percussion

The powerhouse behind the kit whose dynamic playing drives the band's energetic performances. Thomas brings influences from classic rock to modern indie, creating a versatile foundation for the band to build upon.

Favorite breadstick: Classic with Marinara

BAND HISTORY

2021

Formation

Max and Lukas meet at an open mic night in Regensburg and decide to form a band. They recruit Anna and Thomas through mutual friends in the local music scene.

2022

First Shows

The band begins playing small venues around Regensburg and neighboring cities, quickly building a reputation for their energetic live performances.

2023

"Marinara Dreams" EP

Release of their debut EP, recorded in a friend's home studio. The EP receives positive reviews from local music blogs and gets airplay on college radio stations.

2023

First Tour

The band embarks on their first mini-tour, playing shows in Munich, Berlin, and Hamburg, expanding their fanbase beyond their hometown.

2024

"Dough Rising" Album

Release of their first full-length album, recorded at a professional studio in Munich. The album's lead single "Garlic Knots" becomes their breakthrough hit.

2024

Don Bosco Regensburg Show

Their sold-out homecoming show at Don Bosco Regensburg marks a milestone in the band's career, cementing their status in the local music scene.

2025

European Tour

The band announces their most ambitious tour to date, with shows planned across Germany, Austria, Switzerland, and the Czech Republic.
